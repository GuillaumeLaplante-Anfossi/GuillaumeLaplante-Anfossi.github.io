Deformation Quantization

Semester 2, 2025, Syddansk Universitet

Organizers: Alyosha Latyntsev, Guillaume Laplante-Anfossi.

Our goal is to investigate deformation quantization "à la Kontsevich" and closely related topics.


Feel free to reach out to Alyosha or Guillaume if you are interested in participating.


Planning

Room : seminar room of QM

  • Friday February 7, 2025, 2pm - Guillaume: Proof of formality of the little n-disks operad, Notes.
  • Wednesday February 12, 2025, 1pm - Alyosha & Guillaume: The Kontsevich integral + 2 alternative proofs of formality (Tamarkin & Petersen strategies).
  • Friday February 21, 2025, 2pm - Alyosha: Tamarkin's proof of formality of the little discs.
